# Student Companion for Workday

A Chrome extension that adds a live schedule calendar, RateMyProfessors
ratings, and an AI-powered multi-degree planner to your university's Workday
Student portal.

Independent project — not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by
Workday, Inc. or RateMyProfessors.
