## 1. What the extension is

The extension is a study-planning aid that displays your own Workday Student

AI-assisted planning features. It is an **independent project**, not

acting on them. You are responsible for your own enrollment decisions.

On paid plans, AI features are included and run through our service — no API

features pause until it resets at the start of the next month. Automated,
scripted, or shared-account use that abuses the allowance may be throttled or
suspended.

During the free beta only, AI features instead use an Anthropic Claude API key
that you supply. You are responsible for that key, for Anthropic's charges
under it, and for complying with Anthropic's terms; we recommend a key with a
spending limit. The key is stored only on your device.
